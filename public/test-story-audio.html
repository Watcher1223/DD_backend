Bedtime Story — Mood & Emotion Test

Start a Lyria RealTime session and change the music with different themes, moods, and emotions.

Session

Theme comes from your description (say or type it, e.g. “bedtime story in the forest”, “under the sea”).

Use “Play test music” first to confirm your speakers work (plays a short calm WAV from the server).

Change music (theme + mood + emotion)

Click a preset to send POST /api/music/update. Music should transition within ~2 seconds.

Camera → emotion (Gemini Vision)

When the session is running, enable the camera so Gemini can read your expression and adjust the music.

Camera → character (stage vision)

Send camera frames to stage vision. When you (or someone new) appear, the backend generates a story character and optional character card image.

Start the session and subscribe to hear audio. Use a browser that supports Web Audio.
