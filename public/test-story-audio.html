Bedtime Story — Mood & Emotion Test

Start a Lyria RealTime session and change the music with different themes, moods, and emotions.

Session

Theme comes from your description (say or type it, e.g. “bedtime story in the forest”, “under the sea”).

Use “Play test music” first to confirm your speakers work (plays a short calm WAV from the server).

Change music (theme + mood + emotion)

Click a preset to send POST /api/music/update. Music should transition within ~2 seconds.

Camera → emotion (Gemini Vision)

When the session is running, enable the camera so Gemini can read your expression and adjust the music.

Camera → character (stage vision)

Your face is stored first so the character card can look like you. When you appear, the backend generates a character and an optional image. Your face is embedded in that image only when Vertex Imagen subject customization is configured (Google Cloud project with Imagen 3); otherwise you get a text-only illustration.

Camera → doll (story hero)

Point the camera at a doll or stuffed toy (not phones). The backend detects it and sets it as the story protagonist so the narration is about your toy. Use "Next story beat" below to hear and see the story.

Progressive story (narration + image)

Request a story beat to hear spoken narration and see a scene image. Music keeps playing in the background. To switch language, type it in the action (e.g. "Continue the story in Swahili" or "Tell it in Russian"); the next beat and TTS will use that language.

Start the session and subscribe to hear audio. Use a browser that supports Web Audio.
